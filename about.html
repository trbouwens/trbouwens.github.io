About:

   Hi, my name is Tyler, and I am currently a Computer Science Student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I plan
on becoming a software engineer after I reach my bachelors from WPI.

This little website is merely going to be a showcase for my several projects, as well as a starting point for
anyone intrested to get to know my work and interests. Most of my projects stem from a desire to make something
out the blue, and are usually simple or silly in nature. As someone who is still learning, I also know that not every
idea, project, or creation is going to be entirely unique, and some may even be straight from tutorials. However, I
still pursue these projects and stick to them because the process of learning has to start somewhere outside of my
scope of my knowledge, and sometimes following in the footsteps of people who know more than me, or are ready to teach is how I get there.

Projects:
